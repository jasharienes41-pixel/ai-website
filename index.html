❓ Quick Answers

How does the AI itinerary work?

Our AI analyzes your budget, interests, and travel style to build a personalized day-by-day plan in under 30 seconds. It draws from millions of real traveler reviews and local expert knowledge.

Can I change my booking?

Yes! You can modify or cancel your booking up to 48 hours before departure with no fees. Contact us or manage it directly from your confirmation email.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider.

Do you support accessibility needs?

Yes. Our platform is WCAG 2.1 AA compliant, and our coaches can arrange wheelchair-accessible routes, sign language interpreters, and other accommodations. Just mention your needs when booking.
